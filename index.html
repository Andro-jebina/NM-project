Employee Performance & Productivity Analysis

Using Workforce Data to Drive Business Insights

Project Summary

This dashboard presents key trends and analysis on employee productivity using descriptive and exploratory data analytics. Insights are drawn from HR datasets covering demographics, experience, company size, and training hours.

Visual Insights

1. Gender Distribution

Description: Shows the percentage of enrollees by gender.
Purpose: Understand gender representation and diversity in the dataset.

2. Avg Training Hours by Education Level

Description: Displays average training hours across different education levels.
Purpose: Identify if higher education levels receive more training.

3.Relevant Experience

Description: Compares the count of candidates with and without relevant experience.
Purpose: Assess how experienced the workforce is in their field.

4.City Dev. Index by Major Discipline

Description: Visualizes the development level of cities for each discipline.
Purpose: Explore how educational backgrounds relate to urban development.

5. Experience Distribution

Description: Shows how experience is distributed across all enrollees.
Purpose: Identify dominant experience levels in the workforce.

6. Correlation Matrix

Description: Displays correlations between city index, experience, and training hours.
Purpose: Reveal potential relationships between numeric performance factors.

7.Avg City Index by Company Size

Description: Shows average city development index across company sizes.
Purpose: Understand whether larger companies are based in more developed areas.

8. Time Since Last New Job

Description: Plots how long it has been since each employee changed jobs.
Purpose: Analyze job-switching patterns and employee stability.

Key Takeaways

  • Employees with prior experience show higher productivity trends.
  • Training hours positively influence job readiness and retention.
  • Small company employees tend to have more frequent job changes.
